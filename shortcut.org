* Shortcuts for remembering

** org-mode
   - timer
     1. Starts a timer: C-c C-x 0
     2. Record the time since timer: C-c C-x .
     3. Pause and restart a timer: C-c C-x ,
     4. Stop a timer: C-u C-c C-x ,

** macros
   - start a macros: f3
   - finish a macros: f4

** undo-tree
   - undo tree: C-x u
   - show diff: d

** expand-regoin
   - expand choosen or chooseregoin: C-=
